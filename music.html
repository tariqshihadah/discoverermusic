"I am in love" (2016)

"I Am in Love" is a somber exploration of what it's like to fall in love, delving into themes of vulnerability, confusion, and healing.

Recording by Tariq Shihadah at the Candy Corn Room in Urbana, IL
Mixing and Production by Tariq Shihadah
Mastering by Colin Althaus
Artwork by Tariq Shihadah and Laura Shihadah
